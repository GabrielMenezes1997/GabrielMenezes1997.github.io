### **Project - Subway Reviews Analysis**

**Description:** Conducted data analysis and created visualizations to evaluate statements from Subway Restaurant leadership.  
- **Key Insights:**  
  - Evaluated the feasibility of reaching a 4.5/5 rating goal.  
  - Compared Subway’s ratings with competitors such as Jersey Mike's Subs and Panera Bread.  
  - Explored customer review polarization and its impact on rating trends.  

**Technologies:** Python (Matplotlib, Pandas, NumPy, Seaborn), CSV  

**Findings:**
- **Review Polarization:** Confirmed that customers mostly leave reviews when extremely satisfied (5 stars) or dissatisfied (1 star).  
  <img src="https://github.com/GabrielMenezes1997/GabrielMenezes1997.github.io/blob/main/subway_reviews_a1.png?raw=true" alt="Review Polarization" width="600"/>

- **Rating Trends Over Time:**  
  Customer ratings have declined consistently, while review volumes peaked around 2018 and dropped significantly after 2020, reflecting a shift in satisfaction and engagement.  
  <img src="https://github.com/GabrielMenezes1997/GabrielMenezes1997.github.io/blob/main/subway_reviews_a2.png?raw=true" alt="Rating Trends Over Time" width="600"/>

- **Competitor Analysis:**  
  Compared average ratings with key competitors to understand Subway’s relative performance.  
  <img src="https://github.com/GabrielMenezes1997/GabrielMenezes1997.github.io/blob/main/subway_reviews_a3.png?raw=true" alt="Competitor Analysis" width="600"/>

### **Project - Data Visualization Analysis: Exploring Socioeconomic Trends in Brazil**

**Description:** Conducted an in-depth data analysis to explore poverty and socioeconomic trends in Brazil. This project examined relationships between variables such as age, gender, education, employment, and urban vs. rural living.  

**Technologies:** Tableau Public  

**Key Analysis Areas:**
- **Education and Poverty:** Explored how education levels influence poverty rates.  
- **Urban vs. Rural Dynamics:** Analyzed disparities in socioeconomic factors between urban and rural populations.  
- **Workforce Characteristics:** Investigated employment sectors and work permit prevalence.  
- **Demographics and Income:** Examined how age, gender, and ethnicity impact income distribution.  

**Interactive Dashboards:**  
Designed interactive dashboards with bar charts, line graphs, scatterplots, and storyboards to present findings effectively.
