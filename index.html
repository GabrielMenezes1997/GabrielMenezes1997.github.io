Projects

Project — Yelp Inc.

Description: Analyzed raw and large datasets to extract meaningful insights for Yelp.

Technologies: SQL, Power BI

Key Insights:

  • Regional Review Analysis: Identified top and low-performing regions by reviews, supporting targeted marketing and presence improvement.
  • Check-In Patterns: Found peak locations and days for check-ins (e.g., Tuesdays low), informing promo strategy and staffing.
  • Customer Behavior Analysis: Explored review patterns for personalization and satisfaction.

Project — Subway Reviews Analysis

Description: Built visual and statistical analysis to evaluate statements from Subway leadership.

Technologies: Python (Matplotlib, Pandas, NumPy, Seaborn), CSV

Key Insights:

  • Review Polarization: Reviews cluster at 1★ and 5★; mid-range is rare.
  • Rating Trends Over Time: Ratings declined while volume peaked ~2018 and fell after 2020.
  • Competitor Analysis: Compared Subway with Jersey Mike's and Panera Bread.
